About me.

I'm a Perth-based web developer-in-training with a foundational knowledge of HTML, CSS and Javascript.

I currently work in HR for local government and graduated from UWA earlier this year with a BA in linguistics. I am very happy to have discovered coding, as it allows me to put my love of language-learning to more practical use, as well as flex my creative and problem-solving muscles. I hope to eventually become a full-stack web developer and earn my first million by 25.

When I'm not working or studying, I enjoy baking, gardening, swing-dancing, video games and dog-watching. You can find me on LinkedIn here or visit my GitHub profile for more examples of my work.
